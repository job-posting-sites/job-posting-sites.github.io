How to Submit Your RSS Feed Properly

Create a Valid RSS Feed

Ensure your website has a valid RSS feed URL such as:

  • yourdomain.com/feed
  • yourdomain.com/rss
  • yourdomain.com/feed.xml

Choose High Authority Directories

Submit your RSS feed only to trusted and high-authority websites for better SEO results and quality backlinks.

Optimize Feed Title & Description

Use relevant keywords naturally in your RSS title and description to improve search visibility.

Publish Content Regularly

Consistent publishing helps RSS aggregators update your feed frequently and improves audience engagement.
